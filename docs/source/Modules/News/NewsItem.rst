NewsItem
========

Represents a news article.

.. js:attribute:: newsitem.id

    String. A unique identifier for this post.

.. js:attribute:: newsitem.title

    String. The title of this post.

.. js:attribute:: newsitem.content

    String. The body of the post.

.. js:attribute:: newsitem.postTime

    Date. The date and time that the article was posted.

.. js:attribute:: newsitem.user

    NewsUser. The user that posted the article.

.. js:attribute:: newsitem.attachments

    NewsAttachment[]. A list of items attached to this article.
